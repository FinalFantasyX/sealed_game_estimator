Sealed Video Game Survival Estimator

A Fermi Paradox style calculator to estimate how many sealed copies of a video game still exist in the wild.

SealedNow = Sales × BSR × (1−AR)^Y × Π(Fᵢ)

Inputs

Fill in Total Sold and Year Released. Use PS2 FFX as a demo.

Total Sold

Numbers only (e.g., 5300000)

Release Year

4-digit year (e.g., 2001)

Tip: Release Year affects the Baseline Sealed Rate (BSR).
